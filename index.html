About

Studying computer science by building, testing, and writing.

I’m Guilherme, a brazilian CS student and junior developer who likes learning computer science in public. I enjoy taking dense topics like graph search, parsing, and system design and turning them into projects and notes that are easier to revisit later.

I care about clean interfaces, strong fundamentals, and writing that makes technical ideas feel less intimidating. This site is where I collect those ideas.
